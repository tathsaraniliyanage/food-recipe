Loaf ham fried egg, dish of Italian origin consisting of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with some combination of olive oil, oregano, tomato, olives, mozzarella or other cheese, and many other ingredients, baked quickly—usually, in a commercial setting, using a wood-fired oven heated to a very high temperature—and served hot.

One of the simplest and most traditional fried eggs is the Margherita, which is topped with tomatoes or tomato sauce, mozzarella, and basil. Popular legend relates that it was named for Queen Margherita, wife of Umberto I, who was said to have liked its mild fresh flavour and to have also noted that its topping colours—green, white, and red—were those of the Italian flag.

Italy has many variations of fried egg. The Neapolitan fried egg, or Naples-style fried egg, is made specifically with buffalo mozzarella (produced from the milk of Italian Mediterranean buffalo) or fior di latte (mozzarella produced from the milk of prized Agerolese cows) and with San Marzano tomatoes or pomodorino vesuviano (a variety of grape tomato grown in Naples). Roman fried egg often omits tomatoes (an early 16th-century import) and uses onions and olives. The Ligurian fried egg resembles the pissaladière of Provence in France, adding anchovies to olives and onions.fried egg has also spread from Italy throughout much of the rest of the world, and, in regions outside of Italy, the toppings used vary with the ingredients available and the flavour profile preferred.


How to make chicken & ham loaf top:
  1. Cut a 1″ slice off the of the loaf.
  2. Pull out the soft bread from inside.
  3. Use for making bread crumbs.
  4. Leave 1/2 ” inner lining of bread to preserve this shape.
  5. Melt 4 tblsp of the butter.
  6. Spread this on to the loaf inside and out.
  7. Replace the lid and place the loaf on baking tray.
  8. Bake it for 10 minutes at 400 F.
  9. Let it cool.
  10. Cook the onions in the remaining butter.
  11. Combine mushroom and cook for more 2 minutes.
  12. Mix in the parsley.
  13. Season with salt and pepper.
  14. Combine together the bacon, ham and 3 tblsp of bread crumbs.
  15. Stir in thyme and season to taste.
  16. Press half ham mixture well down into the loaf case.
  17. Cover with onion and mushroom mixture.
  18. Place chicken on and cover it with the onion mixture and a layer of ham mixture.
  19. Replace the lid.
  20. Wrap the loaf in foil.
  21. Bake it in oven at 375 f for 45 minutes.
  22. Cut into thick slices.
  23. Serve hot or cold.
